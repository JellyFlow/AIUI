# Mainland China Weather Assistant

This is an intelligent weather application designed specifically for Mainland China, providing accurate and real-time weather query services.

**Core Capabilities:**

*   **🌤 Weather Queries**: Supports real-time weather lookups for provinces, cities, districts, and counties across Mainland China. Simply ask about the weather in a specific city (for example, "How is the weather in Beijing?") to instantly display a polished weather card.
*   **🌡️ Real-Time Conditions and Trends**: Current temperature, humidity, wind, weather conditions, and multi-day forecasts are presented at a glance.
*   **📍 Smart Location**: Automatically detects your current city in Mainland China and provides precise localized weather forecast services.
*   **⚠️ Weather Alerts**: Stay informed with local meteorological warning information to help protect your daily travel plans.

Delivering the most thoughtful and accurate weather information for Mainland China.
